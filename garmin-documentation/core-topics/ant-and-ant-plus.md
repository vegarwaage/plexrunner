# ANT and ANT+ - Connect IQ

## Overview

This page covers wireless sensor protocols ANT and ANT+ in Connect IQ development.

## Key Topics

ANT and ANT+ capabilities include:
- Sensor connectivity
- ANT+ device profiles
- Channel management
- Data transmission
- Power management
- Device pairing

## Common ANT+ Profiles

- Heart Rate Monitor
- Bike Speed/Cadence
- Power Meters
- Fitness Equipment
- And many more
